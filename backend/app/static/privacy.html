Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

At WPGateway, accessible from our application endpoints, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by WPGateway and how we use it.

1. Self-Hosted Data & Privacy Control

WPGateway is a self-hosted software suite. This means that all WhatsApp sessions, user accounts, campaign statistics, and logs are stored locally within your own hosting environment (such as your own PostgreSQL database, Redis server, and local directories). We do not collect, store, or transmit your database records or WhatsApp session files to any external servers.

2. Information We Collect

If you contact us directly for support, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain support assistance for the product.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our software features.
  • Understand and analyze how users interact with our setup instructions to provide better updates.
  • Communicate with you to provide product updates or security warnings.
  • Process payments and transactions securely via our payment processor (WarriorPlus).

4. Log Files

WPGateway follows a standard procedure of using log files locally on your server. These logs record visitors when they access the dashboard. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable and are kept locally in your Docker containers.

5. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, WPGateway uses 'cookies' on the dashboard interface. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information (such as storing your authentication JSON Web Token [JWT]).

6. Payment Processors (WarriorPlus)

We use third-party services for payment processing (e.g. WarriorPlus). We will not store or collect your payment card details. That information is provided directly to our third-party payment processors whose use of your personal information is governed by their Privacy Policy. These payment processors adhere to the standards set by PCI-DSS as managed by the PCI Security Standards Council.
